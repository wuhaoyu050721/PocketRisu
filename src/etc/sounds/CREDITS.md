# Notification Sound Credits

Bundled notification sounds used by 小酒馆's "Notification & Sound" settings.

All files below are sourced from **Mixkit** (<https://mixkit.co/free-sound-effects/>)
under the **Mixkit Free License** — free for commercial use, no attribution
required. This credit file is kept for provenance/traceability only.

Files were downloaded as `.wav` and re-encoded to mp3 (LAME, CBR 128 kbps).
